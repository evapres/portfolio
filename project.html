01

Overview (Problem Statement)

Client Objective

The client wanted to modernize their website to:

  • Showcase the hotel’s aesthetic appeal and unique location in Sifnos.
  • Enable users to book rooms and spa services via external links.
  • Highlight the distinctiveness of the rooms, suites, and the hotel’s spa services.

Problem

The existing website had significant shortcomings:

  • Outdated design that did not reflect the beauty of the hotel or the island.
  • Key features like the spa and hotel details were missing from the homepage.
  • Room pages lacked sufficient photos, clear descriptions of facilities, and proper categorization (rooms vs. suites).
  • The location page did not inspire users with experiences on the island.
  • No dedicated “About Hotel” page to convey its story and charm.

My Role

As the UX researcher, UX designer, and project manager, I was responsible for:

  • Conducting user and client research.
  • Building the sitemap and defining the structure of each page.
  • Designing an intuitive and visually appealing user experience.

02

Empathize (Research Phase)

Stakeholder Insights

Through discussions with the hotel owner, I uncovered key priorities:

  • A clean and modern design that reflects the Cycladic aesthetic.
  • A seamless user journey to find room and spa booking options.
  • Inspiring visitors by showcasing the unique island experiences.

User Pain Points

  • Users struggled to find relevant information about rooms, suites, and spa services.
  • The website lacked visual appeal, which diminished trust and interest.
  • Travelers wanted a better sense of the hotel’s location and its connection to Sifnos.

03

Define (Problem Framing)

Key Insights

  • Users want to be visually inspired by room photos, facilities, and local experiences.
  • Clear categorization of rooms and suites is essential for decision-making.
  • The homepage needs to immediately communicate the hotel’s charm and the spa’s offerings.
  • The location page should inspire potential guests with nearby experiences.

Design Goals

  • Create a modern, visually appealing website that reflects the hotel’s aesthetic.
  • Showcase the rooms, suites, and spa services effectively.
  • Inspire users to explore the hotel and the island.
  • Provide an intuitive navigation structure for seamless exploration.
